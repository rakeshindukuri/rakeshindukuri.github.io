Project 2: Phytochemicals Screening for BRCA Proteins


Aug 2019 - Dec 2019

Associated with Sathyabama Institute of Science & Technology, Chennai

Project Details:

In Silico Screening, Molecular Docking, and Pharmacophore Analysis of Phytochemicals against BRCA1 and BRCA2

Conducted a comprehensive screening and analysis of compounds to identify a lead compound with high potential for binding and docking. Utilized chemsketch to design new structures based on identified subfragments to enhance the lead compound's potential. Performed in Silico molecular docking studies and pharmacophore analysis of phytochemicals against BRCA1 and BRCA2 to evaluate their potential as therapeutic agents.

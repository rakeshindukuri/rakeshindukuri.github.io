Project 4: De novo transcriptome assembly and analysis


Sep 2022 - Dec 2022

Associated with Northeastern University

Project Details:

Performed de novo transcriptome assembly and analysis using Trinity pipeline and BLAST on a Aiptasia genome. Analyzed RNASeq Illumina reads, identified differentially expressed isoforms and genes, and generated annotated reports of differentially expressed transcripts with protein matches, descriptions, and GO terms.
